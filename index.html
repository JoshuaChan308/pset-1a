Why we started Tennis Tales

Tennis has a growing community, and lots of stories to tell. Besides highlighting these stories, it is nice to have a site that can keep you updated on the biggest tournaments for tennis

While browsing other pages such as Yahoo sports, it was somewhat difficult to navigate the page quickly and efficiently when looking for specific stats or stories. Tennis Tales should have easy navigation and more.

What is on Tennis Tales?

Tennis Tales is a website that gives you:

  • Tennis stats
  • Update on professionals
  • Match highlights
  • Stories about tennis near you
